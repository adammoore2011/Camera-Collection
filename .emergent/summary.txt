The user wants to build an iOS app to track and catalogue a vintage camera collection.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Core Functionality:**
  - Track a personal collection of vintage cameras.
  - A Wishlist tab for cameras the user wants to acquire.
  - An Accessories tab for items like lenses, bags, lights, etc.
- **Camera Details:**
  - Name, brand, year.
  - Ability to include pictures of the cameras.
  - Type: Video, Digital, or Manual.
  - Film Format: A comprehensive list including 35mm, Medium/Large Format, Instant (Polaroid, Instax), and various movie formats (Super 8, 16mm, etc.).
- **Data Visualization:**
  - A Stats tab showing a histogram of cameras by their manufacturing year/decade.
- **Customization:**
  - A Settings screen.
  - Ability to choose between multiple color schemes (Dark, Light, Retro, Bright).
  - Ability to select a new app icon from a predefined list of four options.
- **Data Syncing:**
  - User authentication (Google Social Login was chosen) to sync the collection across devices and view it online.
